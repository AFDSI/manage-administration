## **Note About Descriptions:**

Google Cloud projects don't actually have a dedicated "Description" field in the traditional sense. Instead, you use:

**Labels** - Key-value pairs like:
- `purpose: amp-dev-search`
- `environment: development`
- `description: AMP.dev GCS integration`

**Or just update the Project Name** to be more descriptive:
- Current: "AMP-2"
- New: "AMP.dev Search & GCS Integration"
